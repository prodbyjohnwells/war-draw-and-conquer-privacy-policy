Privacy Policy for WAR: Draw & Conquer

Last updated: July 19, 2025

WAR: Draw & Conquer is a mobile card game developed by prodbyjohnwells.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII). However, the app uses third-party services that may automatically collect limited data for the purposes of advertising and analytics. These services may collect:

  • Device information (e.g., device model, OS version, IP address, advertising ID)
  • App usage data (e.g., session duration, interactions)
  • Crash reports and performance diagnostics

The third-party services used may include (but are not limited to):

  • Google AdMob: for displaying ads (personalized or contextual depending on your consent)
  • Unity Ads: for serving video ads and rewarded ads
  • Firebase Analytics: for anonymous analytics and gameplay insights
  • Firebase Crashlytics: for crash reporting and diagnostics

These services operate under their own privacy policies. Learn more here: Google Privacy Policy | Unity Privacy Policy

2. How We Use This Information

Any information collected is used solely to:

  • Serve ads and measure ad performance
  • Improve gameplay experience and fix technical issues
  • Understand overall player behavior through aggregated analytics

3. Children's Privacy

WAR: Draw & Conquer is designed for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

Where applicable, third-party services are configured to comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

If you believe a child has provided personal information through this app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

4. User Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or California, you have rights over your data, including:

  • The right to know what data is collected and why
  • The right to access or delete that data
  • The right to opt out of personalized advertising

Consent prompts may appear within the app if you're in a GDPR or CCPA region. You can also manage ad preferences via your device settings or the “Privacy Settings” button in the app’s Settings menu (if available).

If you would like to request deletion of data collected by third-party services, please refer directly to their privacy policies: Google AdMob Data Deletion and Unity Ads Privacy Policy.

5. Data Security

All data processed by third-party services is transmitted over secure HTTPS connections and stored according to their security policies.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: prodbyjohnwells@gmail.com

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. The latest version will always be available on this page.
